Thank you for visiting our website

We invite you to cruise with us on the beautiful blue waters of Aruba, a tropical island in the Caribbean. We offer amazing yachting day tours and extended charters for visitors to Aruba. Whether it's a birthday party, a wedding reception, anniversary celebrations, graduation parties, or an office party, there is no better way to celebrate the occasion than by cruising along the calm waters of the island of Aruba. It’s time to turn your dreams of a yacht charter vacation into a reality with Exclusive Boat Aruba VIP.

Our beautiful island Aruba, is located in the Caribbean Sea. The tropical climate is attractive for sailing, swimming, and snorkeling all year round as a destination for yacht charters, with an average temperature of 82°F and a gentle summer breeze. Just relax and let our crew take care of you.

We Offer Exclusive Yacht Charter Services

Exclusive Boat Aruba VIP offers Private Yacht Charter Events, Private Fishing Charters, and Daily Tours on the island of Aruba, delivering the ultimate experience on the water aboard a luxury yacht. All our charters are fully crewed charters, outfitted with an experienced and professional captain and crew. Now is the time to book our boat for an amazing day.
Hourly & Daily Yacht Charter

All along the leeward coast of Aruba, the calm, flat sea and the steady trade winds make sailing an ideal activity to add to your holiday experiences.

Set sail for a glorious cruise along Aruba’s scenic coastline. One of the most breathtaking perspectives of our island is the view of our stunning beaches from the sea as you sail along our coastline.

Private Yacht Charter Events

Charter an Exclusive Boat Aruba yacht for the ultimate celebration or special event. We specialize in weddings, holiday events, corporate events, and more!
